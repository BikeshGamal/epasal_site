Who We Are?

ePASAL is a first Nepali Online Fashion Store/Retail of its kind in Nepal. It offers a Wide ranges of products from local and international brands. ePASAL is primarily focused to offer the varieties of Fashion Clothing, Footwear, Accessories and Gifts of Men, Women, Kids and Infants. ePASAL intends to offer the best customer service experience with convenient online shopping systems with various range of products. In addition to efficient after-sales service, It also Provide On-time delivery as well as internationally acceptable payment gateways for customers to use. For instance, major Debit Cards / Credit Cards, American Express, PayPal, eSewa and Cash On Delivery (COD).
